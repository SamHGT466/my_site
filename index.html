About Me

Hey there! I'm Samuel, an IT student from Italy with a deep passion for software development and all things automotive. My journey into tech started years ago, tinkering with my old Samsung Galaxy J3 (2016) — customizing it to the core until there was nothing left to mod. That curiosity eventually led me from ROMs and kernels to code editors and compilers.

Today I write code in C++, Python, JavaScript, and C#, and I'm always looking for new challenges. Whether it's building a school project or experimenting with a new framework, I believe the best way to learn is by building things and breaking them — repeatedly.
